Corporate Services

What can we do for your organization?

Our Services

Codified offers a wide range of services to our corporate partners. We can cover any part of your event hosting needs, ranging from sourcing vendors, planning space, determining strategy and direction, designing a marketing plan, on-site support, community engagement, and more.

Our experience comes from years of planning and organizing events with hundreds of corporate partners, from arrangements as simple as sponsorship activations to full-service white label event production. Our clients and partners have ranged from Fortune 500 companies to local startups, government agencies, non-profits, and everyone in between. Our experience covers nearly all industries, ranging from travel, telecommunications, natural resources, high tech, finance, and more.

Turnkey Event Production

We do it all, start to finish.

Codified's team will work with you from day one to develop your event and its direction, including presenting business cases, developing marketing plans, and partnering with members of the community. From there, we'll contact vendors, design your space, come up with the event flow, and work with you on hacker engagement.

We'll be there in the trenches with you on the day of the event, handling any problem that comes up and making sure everyone is happy. But it doesn't end there - we'll provide you a detailed recap afterwards, including presentations to leadership and comprehensive metrics. We'll even take out the trash.

Individual Services

We'll help you out with what you need.

Sometimes you may just want experts to help advise and support you on certain elements of your event, or your overall strategy. We can help you with any facet.

If you're not quite ready to take the plunge into your own event, we'll advise you on local events to support, and even help represent you at these events. Leveraging a sponsorship opportunity properly can be difficult - our proven experience both hosting sponsors and sponsoring events ourselves can help you make the most of your activation.

Finally, we also serve as a trusted partner for your business's needs. We can offer training, presentations to leadership, and more - whatever you need to support your needs, we're here for you.
